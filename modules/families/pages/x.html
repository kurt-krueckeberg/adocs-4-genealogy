Families in Adams County, Indiana Index of Names of Persons & Firms

The volume Adams County, Indiana Index of Names of Persons & of Firms, available online also well as in the Genealogy Center (call #er 977.201 Ad1aa), is a product of the WPA Historical Survey.

As well as Blakey, Bleeke, Thieme, Schamerloh, etc, the surnames Kruckeberger, Kruekeberg and Kukeberger appear.
