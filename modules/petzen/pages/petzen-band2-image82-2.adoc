= 1789 Christine Louise Krückeberg Birth
:page-role: doc-width

The birth is recorded on image 71, of pages <<image71-136, 136>> and  <<image71-137, 137>> of the Petzen Kirchenbuch 
"Verzeichnis der Getauften, Konfirmierten, Getrauten und Gestorbenen 1785-1827".

== The Birth of April Appears on the Bottom of Page 135

The birth month `April` appears near the bottom of page 70 between entries 18 and 19. It spans the two columns of `geb[oren]. | get[auft].`,
that is, `born | baptized`. The entries at the top of page 71 continue the births in the month of April.
